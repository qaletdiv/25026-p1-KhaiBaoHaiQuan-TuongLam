Manufacturing

The team, expertise, and technologies that support your manufacturing supply chain.

Manufacturers depend on precise, reliable, and efficient supply chains. From raw materials and components to finished goods and specialized equipment, we provide the customs expertise needed to keep production moving without interruptions.

Our specialists assist with HS classification, valuation, tariff treatments, NAFTA/CUSMA requirements, temporary imports, and partner-government agency regulations. We help you avoid delays, reduce compliance risk, and optimize your overall import and export strategy.

Whether you're managing just-in-time production or large-scale industrial projects, we offer the visibility, coordination, and dependable support your operations require for smooth cross-border movement.
